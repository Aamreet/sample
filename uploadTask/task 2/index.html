Bhagat Singh

Bhagat Singh was a hero of the early 20th-century Indian independence movement. He was a freedom fighter who fought fearlessly against British rule, and even in his last moments, he never backed down.

"They may kill me, but they cannot kill my ideas. They can crush my body, but they will not be able to crush my spirit."
- Bhagat Singh
